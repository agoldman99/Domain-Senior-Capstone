ABOUT PLAY

DISCOVERING PLAY PROJECT

There is a disconnect between play research and reality. There is lots of research explaining how and why play is important throughout one’s life, especially in early education. Yet, play has declined in early education classrooms in recent years. DISCOVERING PLAY strives to bridge this gap.

DISCOVERING PLAY is a repository of play activities and play-based education techniques designed to highlight the importance of play and make play more accessible.
